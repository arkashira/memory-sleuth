# Customer Journey Map: Memory-Sleuth

## Phase 1: Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Developer experiences memory leaks or excessive memory usage in their codebase.
- **Friction Points**: Difficulty in identifying the root cause of memory issues.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide educational content on common memory issues and their solutions.
- **Metric**: Number of developers who search for memory optimization tools.

## Phase 2: Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Developer discovers Memory-Sleuth through a recommendation or search.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of detailed information on how Memory-Sleuth can solve their specific memory issues.
- **User Emotions**: Curiosity, skepticism.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer a free trial or demo to showcase the tool's capabilities.
- **Metric**: Number of developers who sign up for a free trial or demo.

## Phase 3: Try
- **Trigger Event**: Developer starts using Memory-Sleuth.
- **Friction Points**: Steep learning curve, difficulty in integrating Memory-Sleuth with existing workflows.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement, frustration.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide comprehensive documentation, tutorials, and customer support.
- **Metric**: Number of developers who complete the trial and provide feedback.

## Phase 4: Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Developer decides to adopt Memory-Sleuth as part of their development process.
- **Friction Points**: Cost, integration with other tools, lack of advanced features.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction, relief.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer discounts for long-term commitments, provide advanced features, and ensure seamless integration with popular development tools.
- **Metric**: Number of developers who purchase Memory-Sleuth and continue using it.

## Phase 5: Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Developer becomes a power user of Memory-Sleuth.
- **Friction Points**: Limited customization options, lack of community support.
- **User Emotions**: Engagement, loyalty.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide customization options, create a community forum, and offer advanced training sessions.
- **Metric**: Number of developers who refer Memory-Sleuth to others and contribute to the community.
