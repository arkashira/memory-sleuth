# Partner Targets
## Overview
As a memory optimization tool, Memory Sleuth will benefit from integrating with various SaaS and APIs to expand its capabilities and reach a broader audience. The following list outlines potential partner targets, prioritized based on their relevance, affiliate/revenue-share opportunities, and integration effort.

## Partner Targets List

### 1. Datadog (Integration Effort: M, Value-Add: Monitoring, Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes)
- **Integration:** Integrate Memory Sleuth with Datadog's monitoring platform to provide users with a comprehensive view of their application's performance and memory usage.
- **Free-Tier Limit:** 5 users, 100MB of logs per day
- **Rationale:** Datadog is a leading monitoring platform, and integrating Memory Sleuth with it will enable users to monitor their application's memory usage in real-time.
